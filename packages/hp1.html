Himachal Pradesh

The Best of Himachal

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the natural splendor of Himachal Pradesh, a haven for nature lovers.
  • Explore the charming hill station of Shimla, visiting iconic landmarks like Kufri, Ridge Maidan, and Mall Road.
  • Embark on an adventure in Manali, renowned for its scenic beauty and thrilling activities like skiing and paragliding.
  • Conquer the majestic Rohtang Pass (subject to weather conditions) and witness breathtaking Himalayan views.
  • Visit the Buddhist Monastery in Manali and the Hadimba Devi Temple, steeped in history and mythology.
  • Seek spiritual solace at the Chamunda Devi Temple and the Buddhist Monastery in Dharamshala, the home of the Dalai Lama.
  • Discover the "Mini Switzerland" of Khajjiar in Dalhousie and enjoy the captivating beauty of Kalatop and Satdhara.
  • Experience the planned city of Chandigarh, boasting stunning gardens and the iconic Rock Garden.

    • Itinerary

    Shimla: Colonial Charm and Panoramic Vistas

  • Begin your exploration in Shimla, the former summer capital of British India. Take a stroll along the bustling Mall Road, a hub for shopping and local delights.
  • Immerse yourself in colonial charm at landmarks like Christ Church and revel in the panoramic views from Ridge Maidan.
  • Explore the nearby hill station of Kufri, known for its scenic beauty and adventure activities like horseback riding.

    • Manali: Adventure Paradise and Spiritual Enchantment

  • Journey onwards to Manali, a paradise for adventure enthusiasts. Embark on an exhilarating excursion to the Rohtang Pass, weather permitting, and witness the majestic Himalayan peaks.
  • Explore the serene Vashisht village, famous for its natural hot springs and ancient temples. Visit the Hadimba Devi Temple, a beautiful shrine dedicated to the wife of Bhima from the Mahabharata.
  • Immerse yourself in the spiritual aura of the newly constructed Buddhist Monastery.

    • Dharamshala A Spiritual Abode

  • Travel to Dharamshala, the spiritual abode of the Dalai Lama. Seek solace at the Chamunda Devi Temple and delve into the rich Tibetan Buddhist culture at the renowned Buddhist Monastery.
  • Explore the scenic Bhagsunag Temple and soak in the breathtaking beauty of the surrounding hills.

    • Dalhousie: Enchanting Hills and Picturesque Landscapes

  • Nestled amidst picturesque hills, Dalhousie offers a captivating escape. Discover Khajjiar, aptly nicknamed the "Mini Switzerland," and marvel at its verdant meadows and serene lake.
  • Explore the natural wonders of Kalatop, a haven for nature walks and outdoor activities. Visit Satdhara, known for its seven springs, and Gandhi Chowk, the bustling heart of Dalhousie.

    • Chandigarh: A Modern Marvel and Artistic Delights

  • Conclude your journey in Chandigarh, a modern marvel known for its well-planned architecture. Explore the captivating Rock Garden, a testament to human creativity, and lose yourself amidst the vibrant blooms of the Rose Garden.
  • Take a stroll along the Sukhna Lake, a scenic haven for locals and tourists alike. Finally, depart from Chandigarh with memories of an unforgettable Himachal adventure.
