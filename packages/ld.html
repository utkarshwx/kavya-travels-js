LADAKH

Package 1

Unforgettable Ladakh: Conquering Khardung La & Beyond

Highlights

  • Explore the historic town of Leh, once the capital of the ancient Ladakhi Kingdom.
  • Immerse yourself in the spiritual aura of ancient monasteries like Shey, Thiksey, and Hemis, each with its unique architecture and history.
  • Witness the stunning beauty of Pangong Tso, a high-altitude lake known for its mesmerizing colors.
  • Conquer the world's highest motorable road, Khardung La, and experience breathtaking Himalayan vistas.
  • Discover the rich cultural heritage and scenic landscapes of Ladakh.

    • Itinerary

    Acclimatization in Leh

  • Upon arrival in Leh, our representative will welcome you and transfer you to your pre-booked hotel.
  • Take time to rest and relax, allowing your body to adjust to the high altitude.
  • In the evening, explore the vibrant Leh market, a kaleidoscope of colors and local crafts.

    • Unveiling Ancient Monasteries

  • Embark on a sightseeing tour of some of Ladakh's most revered monasteries.
  • Visit Shey Gompa, the former summer palace of Ladakhi kings, and marvel at the imposing Shakyamuni Buddha statue.
  • Explore Thiksey Gompa, a magnificent monastery perched atop a hill, offering panoramic views.
  • Journey to Hemis Gompa, the largest monastery in Ladakh, renowned for its vibrant festivals.

    • Likir Monastery and the Indus Valley

  • After breakfast, drive along the Indus River, stopping at the Hall of Fame and Pathar Sahib Gurudwara.
  • Witness the confluence of the Zanskar and Indus rivers, a spectacle of nature's raw beauty.
  • Experience the magnetic pull (an optical illusion) at Magnetic Hill.
  • Reach Likir Gompa, a serene monastery perched on a ridge, and admire its breathtaking views and colossal Buddha statue.

    • Journey to Pangong Tso - High Altitude Lake

  • Rise early and embark on a scenic drive to Pangong Lake, passing through the Chang La mountain pass (18,000 ft).
  • Witness the mesmerizing beauty of Pangong Tso, a high-altitude lake known for its ever-changing colors.
  • Enjoy the stunning vistas of the surrounding mountains and keep an eye out for marmots and kiangs (wild horses) along the way.
  • Return to Leh for a comfortable overnight stay.

    • Conquering Khardung La - The Highest Motorable Road

  • After breakfast, brace yourself for a thrilling journey to Khardung La, the world's highest motorable road.
  • Soak in the breathtaking panoramic views of the Himalayas from this incredible vantage point.
  • Return to Leh and spend the evening exploring the local market, picking up souvenirs of your unforgettable adventure.

    • Farewell from Ladakh

  • Savor a delicious breakfast at your hotel and transfer to Leh airport for your departing flight back to Delhi.

    • FAQ

    What is the best time to visit Ladakh?

    The ideal time to visit Ladakh is generally from June to September when the weather is pleasant and offers clear skies.

    What is the altitude of Leh?

    Leh is located at a high altitude of 11,500 feet. It's important to acclimatize properly to avoid altitude sickness.

    What kind of activities are included in the tour?

    The tour includes sightseeing tours of monasteries, scenic drives through valleys and mountain passes, and free time to explore Leh market.

    Do I need a special permit to visit Ladakh?

    Yes, an Inner Line Permit (ILP) is required for all tourists visiting Ladakh.

    What kind of accommodation is included?

    The tour package includes comfortable hotel stays in Leh.
