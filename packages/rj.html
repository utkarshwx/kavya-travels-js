RAJASTHAN

Package 1: Maharaja's Rajasthan: Palaces & Princely Estates

Highlights

  • Explore City Palace and Jantar Mantar, showcasing Rajput architecture and scientific prowess.
  • Ascend Amber Fort and explore Mehrangarh Fort, offering panoramic city views.
  • Explore the City Palace, take a boat ride on Lake Pichola, and visit Jagdish Temple.
  • Explore Mehrangarh Fort and admire the intricate marblework at Jaswant Thada.
  • Unveil the largest fort in India, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

    • Itinerary

    Unveiling Jaipur's Grandeur

  • Arrive in Jaipur, the "Pink City." Immerse yourself in the City Palace, a treasure trove of Rajput architecture. Marvel at Jantar Mantar, a fascinating astronomical observatory showcasing Maharaja Jai Singh's scientific prowess.

    • Jaipur's Fortresses & Evening Spectacle

  • Ascend Amber Fort, a magnificent citadel and former royal residence. Explore Nahargarh Fort, offering panoramic city views. In the evening, enjoy a captivating light and sound show illuminating the fort's history.

    • Jodhpur's Majestic Forts

  • Journey to Jodhpur, the "Blue City." Explore Mehrangarh Fort, a formidable citadel perched on a hilltop. Visit Jaswant Thada, a beautiful white marble mausoleum, showcasing intricate marble latticework.

    • Scenic Drive & Jain Temple Enchantment

  • Enjoy a scenic drive to Udaipur, the "City of Lakes." En route, stop at Ranakpur Jain Temple, an architectural marvel adorned with intricate carvings and a haven of tranquility.

    • Udaipur's City Delights

  • Explore the City Palace, a grand complex showcasing royal grandeur. Take a boat ride on serene Lake Pichola and visit Jagdish Temple, a renowned Hindu shrine in the heart of the city.

    • Chittorgarh's Fortified Legacy

  • Journey to Chittorgarh, home to the largest fort in India, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore its ramparts, palaces, and towering victory pillar, remnants of a glorious past.

    • Farewell & Fond Memories

  • Depart from Udaipur, cherishing the unforgettable memories of Rajasthan's royal heritage and captivating beauty.


    • Package 2: Tigers & Thar Desert: A Rajasthan Safari

    Highlights

  • Explore the City Palace and marvel at the unique Hawa Mahal, "Palace of Winds“.
  • Embark on safaris in Ranthambore and Sariska, seeking majestic Bengal tigers.
  • Explore Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, a haven for over 350 resident and migratory bird species.
  • Savor delicious Rajasthani cuisine and explore vibrant markets in Jaipur.
  • Visit iconic landmarks like Amber Fort or Jantar Mantar in Jaipur.

    • Itinerary

    Jaipur Enchantment

  • Arrive in Jaipur, the vibrant "Pink City." Begin your exploration at the City Palace, a magnificent complex showcasing Rajput grandeur. Marvel at the Hawa Mahal, the "Palace of Winds," known for its intricate pink facade and unique ventilation system.

    • Chasing Tigers at Ranthambore

  • Journey to Ranthambore National Park, a haven for wildlife and a prime tiger spotting location. Embark on an evening safari, your first glimpse into the park's rich biodiversity, with a chance to encounter majestic Bengal tigers.

    • Ranthambore's Wild Symphony

  • Immerse yourself in Ranthambore National Park with a full day dedicated to wildlife exploration. Experience both morning and afternoon jeep safaris, maximizing your chances of encountering tigers, leopards, and a variety of other wildlife species.

    • A Birder's Paradise: Bharatpur

  • Travel to Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, a haven for birdwatchers. Explore the diverse wetlands brimming with over 350 bird species, including migratory birds and resident avian wonders.

    • Unveiling Sariska's Tigers

  • Journey to Sariska Tiger Reserve, another prime tiger habitat. Embark on a safari, seeking encounters with Bengal tigers and other wildlife inhabiting the park's diverse landscapes.

    • Jaipur Revisited

  • Return to Jaipur and savor a day at your leisure. Explore bustling markets for local treasures, indulge in delicious Rajasthani cuisine, or visit additional cultural landmarks like Amber Fort or Jantar Mantar.

    • Farewell & Fond Memories

  • Depart from Jaipur, cherishing memories of your Rajasthan adventure. Take with you the thrill of wildlife encounters and the captivating beauty of Rajasthan's natural wonders.
