Jammu ANd Kashmir

Package 1

Ultimate Kashmir: Unveiling Valleys & Culture

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir, a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
  • Explore the vibrant city of Srinagar, known for its Mughal Gardens, serene Dal Lake, and unique houseboat stays.
  • Embark on a scenic journey to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold," and witness the majestic Thajiwas Glacier.
  • Ascend to the heights of Gulmarg, a charming hill station renowned for its Gondola ride and world's highest golf course.
  • Discover the idyllic Pahalgam, a "Valley of Shepherds," offering stunning landscapes, saffron fields, and adventure activities.

    • Itinerary

    Arrival in Srinagar - A City Steeped in History

  • Upon arrival in Srinagar, our representative will welcome you and whisk you away to explore the city's enchanting sights.
  • Marvel at the architectural brilliance of the Mughal Gardens, including Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.
  • Seek solace at the Shankaracharya Temple, perched atop a hill and offering panoramic views of the city.
  • Embark on a quintessential shikara ride on the serene Dal Lake, soaking in the tranquil atmosphere and vibrant life around you.
  • Spend the night in a traditional houseboat, a unique experience on the serene waters of Dal Lake.

    • Sonmarg - Meadow of Gold

  • After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Sonmarg, adorned with lush meadows and snow-capped mountains.
  • Witness the majestic Thajiwas Glacier, a natural wonder, and enjoy a thrilling horseback ride (optional).
  • Immerse yourself in the serene beauty of Sonmarg, known as the "Gateway to Ladakh," and explore its captivating landscapes.
  • Return to Srinagar for a comfortable overnight stay at your hotel.

    • Gulmarg - A Paradise Atop the Himalayas

  • After breakfast, drive along the Indus River, stopping at the Hall of Fame and Pathar Sahib Gurudwara.
  • Ascend to new heights on the Gulmarg Gondola, Asia's largest cable car project, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas.
  • Explore Gulmarg, a haven for adventure enthusiasts, known for its world's highest golf course, skiing slopes (seasonal), and scenic beauty.
  • Spend the night nestled amidst the mountains in a cozy hotel in Gulmarg.

    • Pahalgam - Valley of Shepherds

  • After breakfast, embark on a scenic journey to Pahalgam, a charming hill station nestled amidst rolling meadows and snow-clad peaks.
  • En route, stop by the mesmerizing saffron fields and the ancient Awantipur Ruins, remnants of a bygone era.
  • Upon arrival in Pahalgam, check into your hotel and soak in the tranquil atmosphere of the valley.
  • Explore the scenic surroundings on foot or enjoy an optional horse ride, capturing the captivating beauty of Pahalgam.
  • Explore the scenic surroundings on foot or enjoy an optional horse ride, capturing the captivating beauty of Pahalgam.

    • Farewell from Ladakh

  • After breakfast, enjoy some free time to explore Pahalgam at your own pace or indulge in adventure activities like river rafting (optional).
  • Later, we will transfer you to Srinagar Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of your Kashmir adventure.

    • FAQ

    What is the best time to visit Kashmir?

    The ideal time to visit Kashmir is generally from spring (April-May) to autumn (September-October) when the weather is pleasant and offers stunning scenery.

    What kind of activities are included in the tour?

    The tour itinerary includes sightseeing tours, shikara rides on Dal Lake, and optional activities like horseback riding, pony rides, and Gondola rides.

    What kind of accommodation is included?

    The tour package includes comfortable hotel stays in Srinagar and Gulmarg, and a unique houseboat experience on Dal Lake.

    Is there any free time during the tour?

    Yes, there will be free time in the evenings and on some days to explore at your own pace and discover hidden gems of Kashmir.

    Can I extend my trip?

    Absolutely! We can customize the itinerary to extend your stay
