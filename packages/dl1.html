Golden Triangle Explorer: Discover Delhi, Agra & Jaipur

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Indian culture on this classic 5-day Golden Triangle tour.
  • Explore the majestic monuments of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, all UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • Witness the unparalleled beauty of the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
  • Delve into the architectural marvels of Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri, remnants of the Mughal Empire.
  • Discover the vibrant culture and history of Jaipur, the "Pink City" of Rajasthan.
  • Enjoy a comfortable and hassle-free experience with all transportation, accommodations, and guided tours included.

    • Itinerary

    Arrival in Delhi

  • Upon arrival in Delhi, our friendly representative will greet you at the airport. Relax and unwind in comfort as you transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Take some time to settle in and freshen up after your journey.

    • Unveiling Delhi's Treasures

  • Embark on a captivating city tour of Delhi, delving into its rich historical tapestry.
  • Witness the grandeur of the Red Fort, a symbol of Mughal power, and marvel at the architectural brilliance of the iconic Qutub Minar, a towering victory monument.
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Delhi's bustling markets, where you can discover a treasure trove of handicrafts, spices, and local produce.
  • Find serenity amidst the city's vibrant energy by visiting serene temples, offering a glimpse into India's diverse religious traditions.

    • Agra - A Journey Through Mughal Grandeur

  • Travel to Agra, the erstwhile Mughal capital, stopping en route to explore the impressive tomb of Akbar the Great at Sikandra.
  • Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of the Taj Mahal, a monument to love that transcends time.
  • This architectural marvel, adorned with intricate marble carvings and precious stones, is a must-see on any trip to India.
  • Delve into the rich history of Agra Fort, a formidable citadel boasting exquisite architecture and offering stunning views of the surrounding city

    • Jaipur - The Pink City Beckons

  • Journey to Jaipur, the vibrant capital of Rajasthan, stopping at the fascinating deserted city of Fatehpur Sikri, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Explore the Amber Fort, a majestic hilltop fortress that served as the ancient capital of the state.
  • Ascend on elephant back (optional) for a truly regal experience. Immerse yourself in the colorful bazaars and bustling streets of the Pink City.
  • Explore the Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), a unique structure with its pink facade and intricate latticework windows.

    • Farewell and Departure

  • After a delicious breakfast at your hotel, relax and enjoy some free time to explore Jaipur at your own pace.
  • Browse through local shops, savor delicious Rajasthani cuisine, or simply soak in the city's vibrant atmosphere.
  • Later, transfer to the airport for your onward journey, carrying unforgettable memories of your Golden Triangle adventure.

    • FAQ

    What is the best time to visit the Golden Triangle?

    The ideal time to visit the Golden Triangle is generally considered to be from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and sunny.

    What is included in the tour package?

    Our tour package includes comfortable air-conditioned transportation, private guides for sightseeing tours, entrance fees to monuments, and comfortable hotel stays in each city.

    What kind of activities are included?

    The tour itinerary includes guided tours of all major attractions, allowing you to delve deeper into the history and significance of each location.

    Is there any free time during the tour?

    Yes, there will be free time in the evenings and on some days to explore at your own pace and discover hidden gems.

    Can I extend my trip?

    Absolutely! We can customize the itinerary to extend your stay in any city or add additional destinations based on your interests.
