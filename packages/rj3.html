RAJASTHAN

Palace Jewels of Rajasthan: A Luxurious Escape

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the City Palace and Hawa Mahal, remnants of Rajasthan's royal past.
  • Ascend the magnificent fort and relive its historical legacy.
  • Explore the City Palace and enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Lake Pichola.
  • Explore Mehrangarh Fort and admire the intricate craftsmanship of Jaswant Thada.
  • Explore the Jaisalmer Fort and experience the magic of a luxurious desert camp.

    • Itinerary

    A Grand Welcome in Jaipur

  • Arrive in Jaipur, the "Pink City," and check into a majestic palace hotel, reminiscent of Rajasthan's royal heritage. Immerse yourself in history and architecture with visits to the iconic City Palace and the stunning Hawa Mahal, "Palace of Winds.“

    • Amber Fort's Grandeur & Royal Delights

  • Journey to Ranthambore National Park, a haven for wildlife and a prime tiger spotting location. Embark on an evening safari, your first glimpse into the park's rich biodiversity, with a chance to encounter majestic Bengal tigers.

    • Udaipur's City of Lakes

  • Travel to Udaipur, the "City of Lakes," and check in to a luxurious lakeside palace hotel, offering breathtaking views. Explore the City Palace, a treasure trove of Rajput architecture, and enjoy a serene boat ride on Lake Pichola.

    • Enchanting Lake Pichola

  • Take a relaxing boat ride on the picturesque Lake Pichola, soaking in the beauty of Udaipur. Visit Jag Mandir Island, a captivating gem in the heart of the lake, offering a unique historical experience.

    • Jodhpur's Majesty & Tranquility

  • Journey to Jodhpur, the "Blue City." Check into a luxurious fort hotel, and explore the magnificent Mehrangarh Fort, offering panoramic city views. Visit the serene Jaswant Thada, a beautiful white marble mausoleum showcasing intricate craftsmanship.

    • Jaisalmer's Golden Fort & Desert Delights

  • Travel to Jaisalmer, the "Golden City," and explore the majestic Jaisalmer Fort, a golden sandstone marvel. Immerse yourself in the desert experience with a night's stay at a luxurious desert camp, offering unparalleled stargazing and desert charm.

    • Farewell & Enduring Memories

  • Depart from Jaisalmer, cherishing the unforgettable memories of Rajasthan's royal ambiance, captivating cultural experiences, and breathtaking landscapes.

    • FAQ

    Will I be pampered in luxurious accommodations throughout the tour?

    Absolutely! We've carefully curated your experience to include stays in luxurious palace hotels, heritage fort accommodations, and even a premium desert camp for a truly unique stay.

    Are meals included in the package?

    We want you to focus on exploring and savoring the flavors of Rajasthan. A delicious breakfast is served daily to fuel your adventures. Additionally, some special dinners, like the royal dining experience at a heritage palace, are included in the package.

    What kind of amenities can I expect at these luxurious accommodations?

    Unwind and indulge in the finer things! The luxury accommodations boast inviting swimming pools to cool off after a day of exploration. Spoil yourself with a rejuvenating treatment at the on-site spas, savor gourmet dining experiences showcasing regional specialties, and receive top-notch service with concierge services at your disposal.

    Will I have expert guides to lead me through the destinations?

    Yes! Throughout the tour, knowledgeable and passionate guides will be by your side to share the rich history and culture of each location. They'll ensure you get the most out of your experience.

    Is there flexibility to personalize my itinerary?

    Of course! We understand that everyone travels differently. If you'd like to add additional services or activities to your itinerary, feel free to let us know. We'll be happy to arrange them upon request, creating a truly unforgettable Rajasthani adventure tailored to your interests.
