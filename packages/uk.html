Package 1: Himalayan Highlights: Nainital & Ranikhet Charm

Highlights

  • Witness breathtaking Himalayan panoramas, explore iconic sites, and unwind with a scenic boat ride on Naini Lake
  • Discover the serenity of Bhimtal and Sattal lakes, indulge in nature walks, and immerse yourself in the local culture
  • Explore the Kumaon Regiment Museum in Ranikhet, a charming town showcasing colonial-era architecture
  • Visit Chaubatia Gardens, renowned for its orchards and scenic vistas, and explore hidden viewpoints

    • Itinerary

    Unveiling Delhi's Grandeur.

  • Stand in awe of the majestic India Gate and marvel at the architectural wonder of the Qutub Minar.
  • Choose your exploration - delve into the serenity of the Lotus Temple or be captivated by the grandeur of Akshardham Temple.
  • Conclude your day with a delectable journey through Indian flavors at a local restaurant.

    • Tranquility Beckons in Nainital:

  • Embark on a picturesque drive from Delhi to Nainital, absorbing the beauty of the unfolding landscape.
  • Upon arrival, explore the heart of Nainital - Naini Lake. Enjoy a serene boat ride, soaking in the tranquility of the surroundings.
  • Stroll along the bustling Mall Road, lined with vibrant shops and enticing eateries.

    • Exploring Nainital's Treasures:

  • Ascend to Snow View Point via a thrilling cable car ride and witness breathtaking Himalayan panoramas.
  • Visit the Nainital Zoo and come face-to-face with a variety of fascinating creatures from the Himalayas.
  • Seek blessings at the revered Naina Devi Temple, a prominent pilgrimage site.
  • Conclude the day with a visit to Tiffin Top, a popular vantage point offering stunning landscapes.

    • Beyond Nainital's Embrace:

  • Embark on a day trip to Bhimtal and Naukuchiatal, two captivating lakes surrounding Nainital. Indulge in boating, birdwatching, and visit the Bhimeshwar Temple in Bhimtal.
  • Savor the beauty of the return journey from Nainital to Delhi, bidding farewell to the mountains.
  • In Delhi, indulge in some last-minute shopping or explore additional landmarks before your departure. Your tour culminates with a convenient drop-off at the airport or railway station.

    Unveiling the Himalayas: A Spiritual Odyssey through Uttarakhand

  • Embark on a transformative 10-day pilgrimage that unveils the sacred sites nestled within the heart of the Himalayas. This immersive journey traverses the four revered destinations of Uttarakhand - Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath - offering a profound spiritual experience for seekers of inner peace.

    • Unveiling the Source of Sacred Rivers:

  • Yamunotri: Witness the magnificence of Yamunotri Temple, the origin of the holy Yamuna River. Offer prayers and participate in traditional rituals.
  • Gangotri: Delve into the serene beauty of Gangotri, the source of the mighty Ganges. Partake in prayers at the revered Gangotri Temple and soak in the divine aura.

    • Trekking to the Abode of Lord Shiva:

  • Kedarnath: Embark on a challenging yet exhilarating trek to Kedarnath, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. Immerse yourself in the temple's powerful energy and be captivated by the surrounding Himalayan panorama.

    • Experiencing the Divine at Badrinath:

  • Badrinath: Immerse yourself in the divine presence of Lord Vishnu at the Badrinath Temple. Explore the charming Mana Village, considered the last village on the border of India.

    • Unwinding in the Abode of Gods:

  • Haridwar: Witness the captivating Ganga Aarti ceremony on the banks of the Ganges River in the holy city of Haridwar.
  • Uttarkashi: Explore the spiritual town of Uttarkashi, a vital stopover on the Chardham pilgrimage route. Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple and marvel at the breathtaking landscapes
  • Guptkashi: Nestled amidst the Himalayas, Guptkashi serves as a base for the Kedarnath trek. Savor the serenity and prepare for your spiritual ascent.
  • Rudraprayag: Soak in the spiritual significance of Rudraprayag, the confluence of Mandakini and Alaknanda rivers.

    • A Journey of Breathtaking Beauty:

  • Throughout your pilgrimage, be captivated by the majestic Himalayan landscapes. Capture breathtaking snapshots of snow-capped peaks, verdant valleys, and winding rivers.


    • Package 3: Himalayan Rejuvenation: Mussoorie & Rishikesh Wellness Tour

    Highlights

  • Explore the vibrant Mall Road, ascend Gun Hill for panoramic vistas, and discover the cascading Kempty Falls.
  • Unwind in the Eco Park, uncover hidden gems, and savor a delightful picnic amidst lush greenery.
  • Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti, participate in a rejuvenating yoga session, and stroll along the sacred Ganges.
  • Opt for thrilling white-water rafting or invigorating Himalayan treks.
  • Explore the iconic Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula suspension bridges.

    • Itinerary

    Unveiling the Queen of Hills

  • Your adventure commences in Delhi, where you'll meet your guide.
  • We'll then embark on a scenic journey to Mussoorie, aptly named the "Queen of the Hills."
  • Upon arrival, check into your hotel and lose yourself in the vibrant energy of Mall Road in the evening.
  • As dusk settles, savor a delightful dinner at a local restaurant, all while being mesmerized by the captivating mountain views.

    • Unveiling Mussoorie's Treasures

  • Brace yourself for a day brimming with exploration.
  • We'll kick things off with a visit to Kempty Falls, a magnificent waterfall nestled near Mussoorie.
  • Ascend to Gun Hill via the thrilling Mussoorie Ropeway, where breathtaking panoramic vistas await.
  • Later, take a leisurely stroll along Camel's Back Road, a scenic pathway offering stunning natural beauty.
  • After a day filled with exploration, return to your hotel for a well-deserved rest.

    • A Tranquil Escape to Dhanaulti

  • Today, we embark on a captivating day trip to Dhanaulti, a peaceful hill station nestled close to Mussoorie.
  • Immerse yourself in the serenity of the Eco Park and uncover the town's hidden gems.
  • Indulge in a delightful picnic amidst the lush greenery, capturing picture-perfect moments along the way.
  • As the sun begins to set, return to Mussoorie for a relaxing evening and overnight stay.

    • Embracing the Spiritual Heart of Rishikesh

  • Bid farewell to Mussoorie as we journey towards Rishikesh, the spiritual heart of Uttarakhand.
  • Upon arrival, check into your hotel and take a stroll along the sacred banks of the Ganges River.
  • In the evening, witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony at Triveni Ghat, a visual and spiritual spectacle.
  • Cap off the day with a delectable dinner at a charming riverside restaurant.

    • Unveiling Rishikesh's Spiritual & Adventurous Delights

  • Awaken your inner yogi with a rejuvenating yoga session, followed by a hearty breakfast.
  • For the thrill-seekers, Rishikesh offers a plethora of adventure activities, from white-water rafting on the Ganges to invigorating treks in the Himalayas.
  • The afternoon invites you to explore iconic landmarks like Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula, suspension bridges steeped in history and spiritual significance.
  • Return to your hotel for a relaxing evening and overnight stay.

    • A Fond Farewell

  • As our adventure draws to a close, we depart from Rishikesh and return to Delhi.
  • Take a moment to reminisce about the captivating experiences you've collected.
  • Savor a final farewell dinner in Delhi before your onward journey. We'll then transfer you to the airport, ensuring a smooth return home.
