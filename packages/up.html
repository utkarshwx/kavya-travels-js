Divine India: A Tour of Spiritual Discovery

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the spiritual heart of India on a 16-day pilgrimage tour.
  • Visit revered Hindu pilgrimage sites like Mathura, Ayodhya, Varanasi, Chitrakoot, and more.
  • Seek blessings at iconic temples, including the birthplace of Lord Krishna in Mathura and the Kashi Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi.
  • Witness the sacred aarti ceremony on the banks of the Ganges River.
  • Explore the rich history and heritage of prominent cities like Agra, Allahabad, and Khajuraho.

    • Itinerary

    Mathura and Vrindavan

  • Explore the birthplace of Lord Krishna and visit historical temples like Govind Dev Temple and Bankey Bihari Temple.

    • Agra

  • Witness the architectural marvel of the Taj Mahal and explore Agra Fort.

    • Ayodhya

  • Visit the birthplace of Lord Rama and embark on a full-day temple tour, including Ram Janmabhoomi and Kanak Bhavan.

    • Varanasi

  • Experience the spiritual essence of India by witnessing the aarti ceremony on the Ganges and visiting renowned temples like Kashi Vishwanath Temple.

    • Allahabad

  • Explore the holy confluence of the Ganges, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers at Sangam and visit historical sites like Allahabad Fort.

    • Chitrakoot

  • Discover the serene landscapes associated with Lord Rama and Sita.

    • Khajuraho

  • Marvel at the intricate carvings of the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Khajuraho Temples.

    • Ujjain

  • Seek blessings at Mahakaleshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India.

    • Pushkar

  • Visit the holy Pushkar Lake and the revered Brahma Temple.

    • Jaipur

  • Explore the vibrant city of Jaipur, known as the Pink City, and visit historical landmarks like City Palace and Hawa Mahal.

    • FAQ

    What is the best time to visit India for a pilgrimage tour?

    India is a vast country with diverse climates. The ideal time for your pilgrimage tour depends on the specific locations you wish to visit. Generally, the cooler months from October to March are comfortable for travel across most of India.

    What is included in the tour package?

    The tour package typically includes comfortable hotel accommodation, transportation throughout the trip, entrance fees to temples and monuments, and the services of a tour guide.

    Do I need a visa to visit India?

    Yes, most foreign nationals require a visa to visit India. Please check visa requirements with your local Indian embassy or consulate.

    What kind of clothing should I pack?

    It is advisable to pack modest clothing that covers your shoulders and knees when visiting temples and religious sites. Comfortable walking shoes are also recommended.

    Is it safe to drink the tap water in India?

    It is best to avoid drinking tap water in India. Bottled water is readily available and recommended for consumption.
