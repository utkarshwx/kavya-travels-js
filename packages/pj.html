PUNJAB

Package 1

Experience Punjab: A Heritage Tour

Highlights

  • Immerse yourself in the rich history and spiritual significance of Amritsar, visiting the Golden Temple and Jallianwala Bagh.
  • Witness the iconic Wagah Border retreat ceremony between India and Pakistan.
  • Explore the cultural gems of Jalandhar, Ludhiana, and Patiala, including museums, palaces, and sacred sites.
  • Discover the beauty of Chandigarh's Rock Garden, Rose Garden, and Sukhna Lake.
  • Visit Anandpur Sahib, a significant pilgrimage site for Sikhs, and marvel at the Bhakra Nangal Dam.

    • Itinerary

    Amritsar

  • Arrive in Amritsar, visit the Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum, Durgiana Temple, and attend the Wagah Border ceremony.

    • Jalandhar

  • Travel to Jalandhar, explore Pushpa Gujral Science City, Gurdwara Chhevin Padshahi, Devi Talab Mandir, and Rangla Punjab Haveli.

    • Ludhiana

  • Drive to Ludhiana, visit Gurdwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib and Nehru Rose Garden.

    • Patiala

  • Journey to Patiala, explore Qila Mubarak Complex, Sheesh Mahal, and Gurdwara Dukh Nivaran Sahib.

    • Chandigarh

  • Travel to Chandigarh, visit the Rock Garden, Rose Garden, Sukhna Lake, and enjoy shopping in Sector 17 market.

    • Anandpur Sahib

  • Drive to Anandpur Sahib, visit Gurdwara Shri Kesgarh Sahib, Kesgarh Sahib, and Bhakra Nangal Dam. Depart from Chandigarh by train or flight.

    • FAQ

    What is the best time to visit Punjab?

    The best time to visit Punjab generally falls between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for sightseeing.

    What kind of clothing should I pack?

    Punjab experiences hot summers and mild winters. Pack comfortable cotton clothes for summer and light woolens for winter. Modest clothing is recommended when visiting religious sites.

    What is included in the tour package?

    The tour package typically includes comfortable hotel accommodation, transportation throughout the trip, entrance fees to attractions, and the services of a tour guide. Meals may or may not be included, depending on the specific package you choose.

    Do I need a visa to visit India?

    Yes, most foreign nationals require a visa to visit India. Please check visa requirements with your local Indian embassy or consulate.

    What are some things to keep in mind when visiting religious sites in Punjab?

    It is important to dress modestly and cover your head when entering Sikh Gurdwaras. Remove your shoes before entering any holy site.
